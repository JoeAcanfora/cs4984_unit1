Millions affected by floods in eastern China








Please turn on JavaScript. Media requires JavaScript to play.
































19 June 2011
Last updated at 08:43 ET

Help


More than two million people are now reported to have been affected by severe floods in eastern China. 
Some of those who were evacuated are now returning home, many crops have been destroyed and businesses damaged. 
The torrential rains have killed at least 170 people.
Jonathan Josephs reports.
