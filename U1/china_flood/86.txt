Tire Maker Will Invest $1.2 Billion in South Carolina Plants

The company also said Wednesday that it's boosting the size of a previously announced expansion of an existing plant nearby. All told, the company has pledged to invest $1.2 billion in the projects and create more than 850 jobs by 2020.
